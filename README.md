# Churn-Prediction

Customer Churn Prediction helps businesses **identify customers who are likely to stop using a product or service**. By predicting churn in advance, companies can take actions to retain valuable customers.

### **Uses of Customer Churn Prediction**

* Identifies customers who are likely to leave.
* Helps businesses improve **customer retention**.
* Reduces customer loss and increases revenue.
* Enables personalized offers and discounts for at-risk customers.
* Improves customer satisfaction through timely support.
* Assists marketing teams in designing targeted retention campaigns.
* Supports data-driven business decision-making.
* Reduces the cost of acquiring new customers by retaining existing ones.

### **Industries That Use Churn Prediction**

* Telecommunications
* Banking and Finance
* E-commerce
* Subscription Services (Netflix, Spotify)
* Healthcare
* Insurance
* SaaS (Software as a Service)
